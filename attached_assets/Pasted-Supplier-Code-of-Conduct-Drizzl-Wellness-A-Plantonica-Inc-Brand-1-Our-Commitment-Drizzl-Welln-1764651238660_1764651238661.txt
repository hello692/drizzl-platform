Supplier Code of Conduct

Drizzl Wellness | A Plantonica Inc. Brand

1. Our Commitment

Drizzl Wellness is committed to responsible sourcing, ethical operations, and respect for people and the planet. We expect the same commitment from all suppliers, manufacturers, and business partners (“Suppliers”).

Our standards are aligned with:

The Universal Declaration of Human Rights

The International Labour Organization (ILO) Conventions

The UN Global Compact Principles

This Code defines the minimum requirements for doing business with Drizzl Wellness and supplements all applicable laws, regulations, and contractual obligations.

2. Scope & Supplier Responsibility

This Code applies to:

All Suppliers

Their employees

Affiliates

Subcontractors

Upstream supply partners

Suppliers are responsible for:

Compliance with this Code

Communicating these standards in local languages

Maintaining internal controls to ensure adherence

3. Human Rights & Labor Standards

Suppliers must:

Respect Human Rights and avoid any form of abuse or exploitation

Prohibit Forced Labor & Human Trafficking

Prohibit Child Labor and protect young workers

Ensure Freedom of Association & Collective Bargaining

Eliminate Discrimination & Harassment

Provide Legal Wages, Safe Hours & Fair Benefits

Maintain Safe & Healthy Work Environments

Zero tolerance applies to:

Physical abuse

Sexual exploitation

Psychological coercion

Retaliation against whistleblowers

4. Environmental Protection

Suppliers must:

Comply with all environmental laws and permits

Reduce waste, emissions, and resource consumption

Manage hazardous chemicals responsibly

Promote recycling and sustainable materials

Support climate-responsible operations aligned with the Paris Climate Agreement

5. Ethical Business Conduct

Suppliers must:

Deliver products and services that meet all safety and quality standards

Avoid conflicts of interest

Comply with antitrust and fair competition laws

Maintain accurate records and documentation

Prohibit bribery, corruption, extortion, and fraud

Ensure full sourcing traceability and transparency

Protect confidential information & intellectual property

Comply with data protection and information security laws

6. Monitoring, Audits & Compliance

Drizzl Wellness reserves the right to:

Conduct audits and on-site inspections

Require corrective actions

Terminate any business relationship for non-compliance

Violations of this Code constitute a material breach of contract and may result in immediate termination and legal action.

7. Continuous Improvement

Suppliers are expected to:

Go beyond minimum compliance

Adopt global best practices

Continuously improve labor, environmental, and ethical performance

Effective Governance

This Supplier Code of Conduct is enforced under the authority of Drizzl Wellness and Plantonica Inc. Executive Management.
